# Coastline Charging – Roadmap

**A real-world EV charging service.**

Coastline Charging began as a mobile DC fast charging service in Orange County, California. In two and a half years, we've delivered over 100,000 miles of EV range across thousands of sessions — all without public funding or venture capital.

We own our equipment, run our fleet, and stay employee-owned and owner-operated. We've built and stress-tested the systems ourselves: dispatch, routing, pricing, and ops. From here, we're focused on growing the service carefully across Southern California.

---

## ✅ Phase 1 — Core Ops Foundation (Completed)
- Launched mobile DC fast charging across Orange County
- Refined dispatch, routing, and pricing systems in live environments
- Proved unit economics across both consumer and fleet segments
- Delivered 100,000+ miles of EV range in thousands of sessions

---

## 🌊 Phase 2 — The Southern California Brand (In Progress)
- Expand service into Los Angeles and San Diego counties
- Deepen fleet relationships across Southern California
- Keep raising the bar on reliability, response time, and on-site experience
- Grow our mobile charging fleet to match demand
- Stay employee-owned and owner-operated through the next stage of growth

The aim: become the regional mobile charging service Southern California turns to as the area grows from hundreds of thousands of EV drivers today to millions in the years ahead — built carefully, run reliably, and owned by the people doing the work.

---

## 🌐 rohm — the Marketplace Layer

The broader, category-level work — a public marketplace for mobile charging operators, fleet APIs, a driver app, roaming, and settlement — is being built separately at **[rohm](https://rohmev.com)**.
